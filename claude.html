About Karen

Hi, I'm Karen Shrira Steinitz, a San Francisco-based artist specializing in graphite, colored pencil, and ballpoint pen portraits. With years of experience, I capture the unique personalities and special bond between pets and their owners through meticulously detailed artwork.

Each portrait takes approximately 20-40 hours to complete, depending on size and complexity. I work exclusively with lightfast, professional grade colored pencils that resist fading, ensuring your portrait remains vibrant for generations.

My process begins with studying your pet's photos to understand their unique characteristics. Then I layer colors gradually, building up texture and depth to create a lifelike representation full of personality.
